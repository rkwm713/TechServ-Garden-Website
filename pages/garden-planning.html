Garden Planning Tips

Consider Sun Exposure

Map the sun patterns in your garden before planning. Most vegetables need 6-8 hours of direct sunlight daily.

Plan for Water Access

Ensure your garden design includes easy access to water sources to simplify irrigation.

Consider Plant Height

Place taller plants on the north side of your garden to prevent shading shorter plants.
