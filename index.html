Garden Features

Interactive Planning

Design your garden plot with our drag-and-drop layout editor. Choose from plants suited to East Texas climate and get personalized growing tips.

Task Management

Track garden tasks, sign up for volunteer opportunities, and coordinate with other gardeners to keep our community garden thriving.

Real-time Garden Map

View the current state of the garden, including plant inventory, maintenance alerts, and available plots for new plantings.

Knowledge Base

Access gardening resources specific to East Texas, including planting guides, pest control tips, and seasonal advice from experienced gardeners.

Community Events

Join workshops, social gatherings, and volunteer days. Share your gardening journey through photos and discussion forums.

Resource Tracking

Monitor garden expenses, inventory supplies, and track sustainability metrics to ensure efficient use of resources.

  • Spring Planting Has Begun!

    March 5, 2025 Garden Admin

  • New Irrigation System Installed

    March 2, 2025 Engineering Team

    The Engineering team has installed a new drip irrigation system in Sections A and B. This system will reduce water usage by approximately 30% while ensuring plants receive consistent moisture.

  • Winter Harvest Complete

    February 28, 2025 Harvest Team

    We've completed our winter harvest with a total of 75 pounds of produce! Kale, spinach, and winter squash were distributed to the cafeteria and participating gardeners.
